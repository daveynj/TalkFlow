Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Product Name: TalkFlow AI: Student Tutor
Document Owner: Dave Jackson
Target Launch: Q3 2026
Status: Draft / Prototype Phase

1. Executive Summary

Vision: To democratize high-quality, CEFR-aligned English language education by providing students with an affordable, interactive, and proactive AI tutor available 24/7.
Background: Building on the success of the Planwise ESL B2B lesson generator, this B2C platform allows students to independently consume interactive lessons. The AI acts as a virtual Dave Jackson—guiding, speaking, correcting, and scaffolding the learning experience just like a seasoned human teacher would.

2. Target Audience

Adult Learners: Professionals and university students aiming to improve fluency and business English (A2 - C1 levels).

Young Learners (Parents as buyers): Parents looking for supplemental English practice for their children, building on traditional kindergarten/school education.

3. Core Features & Requirements

3.1. The Homepage & Onboarding

Hero Section: Clear value proposition ("Master English with your personal AI Tutor").

Interactive Demo: A 30-second mini-lesson right on the homepage so users experience the AI voice and interaction immediately.

CEFR Placement Test: A short, dynamic 5-minute quiz upon signup to determine the user's starting level (A1 to C2).

Dashboard: Displays user progress, current CEFR level, daily streaks, XP earned, and recommended next lessons.

3.2. Proactive AI Tutor Interaction

Text-to-Speech (TTS): The AI must speak the lesson script out loud using natural, conversational voices (e.g., via Web Speech API or ElevenLabs integration).

Proactive Teaching: The AI doesn't just wait; it prompts. If a student lingers on a question for >30 seconds, the AI verbally offers a hint (e.g., "Remember, we are looking for a word that means 'brothers and sisters'.").

Speech Recognition (STT): Students can click a microphone to read sentences out loud. The AI provides real-time pronunciation feedback.

3.3. Lesson Architecture (Powered by Planwise ESL Engine)

Lessons will be generated dynamically based on the student's level and interests, following Dave's structured methodology:

Warm-up: Image-based discussion prompts.

Vocabulary: Interactive flashcards with audio playback, definitions, and example sentences.

Reading: Texts tailored to exact CEFR levels with target vocabulary highlighted.

Interactive Exercises:

Fill in the Blanks: Contextual grammar/vocab practice.

Sentence Unscramble: Drag-and-drop words to form correct syntax.

Multiple Choice Knowledge Checks.

3.4. Gamification & Retention (Added Features)

XP & Streaks: Earning points for completed modules and maintaining daily learning streaks to drive daily active usage (DAU).

Spaced Repetition System (SRS): The AI remembers which vocabulary words the student struggled with and proactively re-introduces them in future warm-ups.

Certificates: Downloadable completion certificates when a student levels up (e.g., moving from B1 to B2).

4. Monetization Strategy (B2C SaaS)

To build a sustainable, recurring revenue business, we will implement a "Freemium" SaaS model using Stripe for payment processing.

Tier 1: Free Basic (Lead Generation)

Includes the Placement Test.

Access to 2 free starter lessons per CEFR level.

Standard AI voice.

Goal: Let them experience the value and hit a paywall when they want to continue their "streak".

Tier 2: TalkFlow Pro ($12 - $15 / month)

Unlimited access to the entire lesson library.

Proactive AI voice guidance.

Access to all interactive exercises (Unscramble, Fill-in-the-blanks).

Progress tracking dashboard.

Tier 3: TalkFlow Premium / "Talk with Dave" Hybrid ($49 - $99 / month)

Everything in Pro.

Advanced AI Speech Recognition for pronunciation feedback.

Human-in-the-loop: 1x 30-minute live session per month with a real tutor (Dave or contracted teachers) to practice what the AI taught them. This leverages your existing "Talk with Dave" business as a premium upsell.

5. Technical Stack Requirements

Frontend: React.js / Next.js, Tailwind CSS for responsive mobile-first design.

Voice Integration: Web Speech API (Free MVP) moving to OpenAI TTS or ElevenLabs for premium, ultra-realistic voices.

Backend/Database: Firebase or Supabase for user authentication, saving progress, and storing lesson states.

Payments: Stripe Subscriptions.

Content Generation: Connecting the existing Planwise ESL AI generation engine via API to populate the student app.

6. Phase 1 Milestones (MVP)

Month 1: Develop Homepage, Auth, and Stripe integration.

Month 2: Build the Student Lesson Canvas (incorporating the UI prototype).

Month 3: Integrate Web Speech API for AI voice and build the Placement Test.

Month 4: Beta testing with 50 existing "Talk with Dave" students.
